Outline is a tool that generates robust CSS grids to your exact specification.

To get going simply fill in the form to the right and hit generate. It works best if your width can divide by your column count and your margin width is a multiple of your column count.

A good example would be 960px width, 16 columns and 32px margin. You could swap to 12 columns but your margin would need to change to a multiple of 12, such as 24px.

If you do not meet these guidelines then you will get decimal widths (126.3, 50.89, 90.324) which may mess up in some old browsers. This is because browsers round differently.

Your generated grid will be displayed below. All you have to do then is copy and paste it into a file or save the target of the download link.
